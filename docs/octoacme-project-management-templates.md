# OctoAcme — Project Management Templates & Checklists

## Purpose
Provide reusable templates and checklists that make OctoAcme project execution repeatable across teams.

## When to use
- During kickoff to seed project artifacts quickly.
- During each phase transition to confirm readiness.
- During onboarding so new contributors can follow one standard template set.

## Phase handoff checklist
- [ ] Initiation outputs approved and linked in repo
- [ ] Planning backlog prioritized with estimates and owners
- [ ] Execution tracking board and reporting cadence active
- [ ] Release checklist completed with rollback plan
- [ ] Retrospective action items captured with owners and due dates
